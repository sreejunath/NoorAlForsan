Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and business operations.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for wholesale orders?

Our MOQ varies by product category. Please use the contact form or WhatsApp link to send a Wholesale Inquiry, and our team will provide you with the specific details and catalog.

Do you ship outside of the UAE and GCC?

Yes, we offer international bulk export services. Shipping rates and timelines depend on the destination and order volume. All details will be provided during the inquiry process.

Are your products cruelty-free or vegan?

We are committed to ethical sourcing. The majority of our premium collection is cruelty-free. Specific vegan and organic certifications are listed in the detailed product specifications, available upon request.

Can I visit your location?

Our main operations are handled via online and direct sales consultations. If you require a physical meeting for a large wholesale partnership, please schedule an appointment via the contact form.
